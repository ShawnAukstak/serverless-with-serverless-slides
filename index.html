FOCUS

  • Don't spend time managing infrastructure.
  • Don't spend time writing standard components (Auth, API routing/validation).
  • Write code and design architecture to solve business problems.
  • Allows for innovation and faster movement.

SAVINGS

Many studies suggest most servers are less than 20% utilized. In serverless you only pay for computing you actually use.

SCALIBILITY

Scaling up and down is automatic and managed by the provider.

PROTOTYPING

  • Little / no cost since you only pay for what you use.
  • First million requests are free with AWS Lambda.

OTHER BENEFITS

  • Multi-Region / Canary Deploys
  • Polygot
  • Greener computing

DRAWBACKS

  • Function Limitations (cold start / max duration)
  • Vendor lock-in
  • Do not have access to special hardware
  • Increased remote calls
  • Immature Tooling (Testing, deploying, debugging, monitoring)
